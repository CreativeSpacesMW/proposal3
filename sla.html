This is a 3-month intensive project starting from the date of the Soft Launch.

  • Month 2 (The Trusted Lion): Transition to operational transparency and B2B credibility
  • Month 3 (The Lion's Legacy): Staff training (CapCut, Meta Business Suite) and final handover of digital controls

  • Uptime: The ecosystem will maintain a 99.5% availability rate
  • Security: SSL encryption across all sub-domains (Vercel deployment)
  • Support: Priority technical support for the duration of the 3-month marketing campaign
  • Response Time: Critical system bugs will be addressed within 6 hours during the "Blitz" phase
